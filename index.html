About Me

ONESart is a Design and Illustration Studio based in Magelang, Indonesia. It is an artist name of Egi Setiawan a mastermind behind the studio. Featuring a highly skilled drawing on a digital touch making the studio different than the other. Established in 2009, onesart has worked for many projects and clients from all over the world. They are coming from the music industry, creative, printing, clothing company, beverage and food companies, skateboard, snowboard and others. Producing the logo design, t-shirt design, poster design, skateboard / snowboard graphic, Album / EP design, Character design, Mascot etc. Need a help in design and illustration, do not hesitate to contact me.
